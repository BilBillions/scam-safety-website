5 Red Flags You're Talking to a Scammer

Published: June 2025

Online scammers often use emotional manipulation and urgency. Here are 5 red flags to look out for:

  1. They avoid video calls and always have an excuse.
  2. They fall in love very quickly and use emotional language.
  3. They claim to be overseas or in the military to avoid meeting.
  4. They ask for money, gift cards, or crypto “just this once.”
  5. They pressure you to move the conversation off the dating platform quickly.

If you notice any of these signs, take a step back and talk to someone you trust. Scammers prey on silence and hope. You're not alone.
