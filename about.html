About Bil Miracle

Your Scam Safety Advocate

Protecting You from Online Dating Scams and Phishing Fraud

Hi, I’m Bil Miracle, your Scam Safety Coach and founder of ScamSafetyCoach.online.

My journey began in a place I’m not proud of: I was once involved in online scams. It’s difficult to admit — but it’s part of my story. And it’s the reason I’m here today.

Years ago, I participated in romance scams, phishing schemes, and fake investment setups. But one day, everything changed. I thought of my own mother — and asked myself, “How would I feel if someone did to her what I was doing to others?”

That moment shook me. It made me stop, reflect, and choose a different path.

Through deep self-reflection and a commitment to change, I walked away from that world — and turned my experience into a mission to protect others from the very harm I once caused.

Today, I teach people how to recognize the emotional and psychological tactics scammers use — especially in online dating, phishing, and crypto fraud. My goal is to empower you with knowledge so you can protect yourself emotionally, financially, and confidently support your loved ones who may be at risk.

This site is more than just information. It’s a space for awareness, truth, and healing — built on real experience and sincere purpose.

If you're reading this, you've already taken a powerful step toward safety. You're not alone anymore. I’ve lived both sides of the story — and I’m here to guide you through it.

Let’s stay safe — together.

– Bil Miracle
